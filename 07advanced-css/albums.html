Freudian (2017)

Download Free Flac

Daniel Caesar's debut album, *Freudian*, was released in 2017 to critical acclaim. The album blends R&B, soul, and alternative influences to create a sound that is both contemporary and nostalgic. The record features standout tracks like "Get You" (featuring Kali Uchis) and "Best Part" (featuring H.E.R.), both of which helped to elevate Caesar’s career to international recognition. The album explores themes of love, heartbreak, and personal introspection, which resonated deeply with listeners.

Key Tracks:

  • "Get You" (feat. Kali Uchis)
  • "Best Part" (feat. H.E.R.)
  • "We Find Love"
  • "Blessed"

CASE STUDY 01 (2019)

*CASE STUDY 01* is Daniel Caesar’s sophomore album, released in 2019. This project marks a departure from the sound of *Freudian*, embracing a more experimental and eclectic approach to R&B. The album features collaborations with artists like Brandy and Sean Leon, and highlights Caesar's growth as an artist both sonically and lyrically. It was a bold step forward, with themes of self-reflection, vulnerability, and the complexities of modern relationships.

Key Tracks:

  • "Love Again" (feat. Brandy)
  • "Are You OK?"
  • "Superposition" (feat. Sean Leon)
  • "Frontal Lobe Muzik"

NEVER ENOUGH (2023)

Released in 2023, *NEVER ENOUGH* continues Daniel Caesar's journey through R&B, while incorporating more introspective, melancholic tones. The album is marked by its sophisticated production, emotional depth, and more minimalist arrangements. Caesar explores themes of isolation, love, and personal growth, with tracks that showcase his evolving voice and songwriting ability.

Key Tracks:

  • "Toronto 2014"
  • "Always"
  • "Ocho Rios"
  • "Disillusioned"
